me

i did my arts foundation course at central st. martins. chose graphics. enjoyed drawing.

i then went on to graduate in architecture at the university of portsmouth.

from that i worked in town planning and urban development.

i then enjoyed working as a creative in advertising & learning to code.

subsequently i have progressed by undertaking a postgraduate degree in spatial planning & development at the university of reading.

after that, all i can say with confidence is i shall enjoy not working in a bank.
